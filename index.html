Welcome to Kim's Travel Adventures

Traveling has always been something I want to do more of. I like learning about different places, trying new food, and seeing what everyday life looks like in other parts of the world.

On this website, I collect ideas, notes, and dream itineraries for some of the cities I’d love to visit one day. For now, it’s a place to learn and imagine.

Top Places I Want to Visit

Beyond these places, there are dozens of other locations on my list: bustling cities, quiet coastal towns, and mountain villages.

Tokyo, Japan

for the culture, food, and city energy.

Tokyo seems exciting because it mixes old traditions with bright, modern city life. I want to try the food, see famous places, and experience how busy and lively the city feels.

Paris, France

mainly for the art and landmarks.

Paris feels beautiful and full of history, with museums, famous buildings, and charming streets. I want to see the sights, enjoy the cafés, and experience its relaxed atmosphere.

Bali, Indonesia

because the scenery looks peaceful and relaxing.

Bali seems calm and welcoming, with beaches, rice terraces, and nature everywhere. I want to enjoy the scenery, slow down, and see what everyday life is like there.
